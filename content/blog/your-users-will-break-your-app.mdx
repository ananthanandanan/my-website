Real users don't behave like test cases. They click in the wrong order, paste huge payloads, and close the tab mid-request. That's where the real learning begins.

## Embrace the chaos

If your app never breaks in production, you're either not shipping or not listening.

## What we did

We added better error boundaries and started logging client-side errors. Game changer.
